# Pieces ₽

Also known as money or credits. This is the main currency used to purchase items from the item shop. The symbol is denoted as `₽` and it has no relation to real world money.

<Callout>
  **Legacy:** This used to be called credits in Titan 2, denoted with the `$`
  symbol
</Callout>
